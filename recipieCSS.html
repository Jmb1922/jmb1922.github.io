Crêpe Recipie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of melted butter

Directions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour and eggs.
  2. Gradually add in the milk and water
  3. Stir to combine
  4. Add the salt and butter
  5. Beat mixture until smooth
  6. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat
  7. Pour or scoop around 1/4 cup of batter onto the gridle
  8. Tilt the pan with a circular motion so the batter coats the surface of the pan evenly
  9. Cook the crêpe for about 2 minutes or until the bottom is light brown
  10. Loosen with a spatula
  11. Turn and cook on the other side
